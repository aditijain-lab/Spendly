Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address. As you use the app, we also store the expense data you enter — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions — so we can display it back to you.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to operate Spendly: authenticating your account, displaying your transactions, and generating summaries and category breakdowns. We do not use your personal or financial data for advertising, and we do not sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is only accessible through your authenticated account. We retain your information for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your expense data with third-party advertisers or data brokers. We may rely on trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the application; these providers do not have independent rights to use your data beyond what is necessary to provide their service to us.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on the Spendly website.
